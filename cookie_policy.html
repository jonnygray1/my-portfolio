What are cookies?

A cookie is a small file of letters and numbers that we store on your browser or the hard drive of your computer. Cookies contain information that is transferred to your computer's hard drive.

How we use cookies

We only use Google Analytics to track how visitors use our site. This information is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally. We use this information to improve our website and the services we offer.

Managing cookies

You can manage cookies by adjusting the settings in your browser. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this website.
